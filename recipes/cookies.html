Chocolate Chip Cookies

Bakery Style Chocolate Chip cookies in 30 minutes!

Ingredients
  • 380 grams all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 sticks unsalted room temp butter
  • 100 grams granulated sugar
  • 247 lightly packed brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 large eggs
  • 340 grams semisweet chocolate chips
Steps
  1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees and line baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Combine flour,baking soda, and salt
  3. Beat butter,granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy. Add vanilla and eggs.
  4. Gradually beat in the flour and chocolate chips
  5. Wrap dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 24 hours
  6. Let dough sit at room temperature just until it is soft enough to scoop
  7. Use cookie scoop to drop onto baking sheets
  8. Bake for 11-13 minutes or until golden brown
  9. Let cool for 5 minutes
  10. Enjoy!
