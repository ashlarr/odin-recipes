Brioche Sandwich

Wait...isn't this vaugely familar?

Ingredients
  • The finest Brioche Bread
  • Pureed Nut Spread
  • Grape Relish Reduction
  • Butter Knife
Steps
  1. Remove 2 slices of bread from plastic bag, really...it is brioche
  2. Pour liquid off of imported nut spread
  3. Get grape relish reduction from fridge, ignore date on jar
  4. Spread nut spread on left slice. Left ONLY.
  5. Spread grape relish reduction on left side. Ignore previous direction. Definently left.
  6. Smash slices together
  7. Leave dirty butter knife on edge of kitchen sink
  8. Roll bread bag up and set tail under loaf
  9. Ignore jar of relish and nut butter on counter
  10. Enjoy!
